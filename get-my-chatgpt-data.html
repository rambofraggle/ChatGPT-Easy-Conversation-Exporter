Export My ChatGPT Conversations

Follow the one-time setup below. After that, exporting your conversations takes a single click — no installs, nothing to type.

1

Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible

Chrome or Edge (Windows): Press Ctrl + Shift + B

Chrome or Edge (Mac): Press ⌘ + Shift + B

Safari (Mac): Click View in the menu bar → Show Favourites Bar

2

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

Click and hold the green button, then drag it up to your bookmarks bar and drop it there.

⬇️ Export My ChatGPT Data

👆 Drag that green button up to your bookmarks bar

Your bookmarks bar will look like this: Gmail Drive ⬇️ Export My ChatGPT Data
⚠️ Dragging not working? Right-click the green button and choose "Bookmark this link" or "Save link as bookmark". When asked where to save it, choose Bookmarks bar (or Favourites on Safari).

✅ Setup complete — here's how to export:

1. Go to ChatGPT and log in.
2. Click the "⬇️ Export My ChatGPT Data" bookmark.
3. A progress bar appears. When done, a ZIP downloads automatically.
